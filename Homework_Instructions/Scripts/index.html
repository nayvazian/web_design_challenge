HomeWeather Data Visualization

The intention of this webpage is to allow users to seemlessly move from page to page to view multiple data visualizations. On the right of the page is a dropdown menu with all of the links. At the bottom of the page you can preview the figures and click on their respective links to view a full sized figure. You can return to the home page by clicking the home button in the top left.
